Protein Ice Cream

Ingredients

  1. Full Fat Coconut Milk
  2. Protein Powder (vanilla protein powder works)
  3. Granulated Sweetener of choice/dates/coconut sugar
  4. Vanilla Extract

Directions

  • Prep - Before making the ice cream, place your pan in the freezer for at least an hour.
  • Blend - Add your chilled coconut milk to the blender and blend for a minute or two(texture needs to be silky and smooth)
  • Add your protein powder and sweetener of choice and blend until smooth and creamy
  • Stir and Chill - Place the contents into the pan and then place in the freezer. In the first hour, stire the ice cream every 20 minutes.
  • Serve - Remove the protein ice cream from the freezer for 10-15 minutes to thaw a bit, then enjoy!
