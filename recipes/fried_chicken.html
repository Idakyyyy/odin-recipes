Fried Chicken Recipe

Your chicken should look close to this in the end!

The following contain three stages and it's ingredients as follows:

Spice Prep:

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp dreid oregano
  • 1/2 tbsp cayenna pepper

The Brine:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • salt/kosher salt

Coating Stage:

Ingredients

  • any pieces of chicken
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 4 cups vegetable shortening
